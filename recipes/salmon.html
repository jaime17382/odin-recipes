Baked Salmon Recipe

Description

This baked salmon recipe is a perfect late dinner recipe that is easy to make and amazingly delicious

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh salmon filets of about 6 oz each
  • start by brushing our salmon filets with a little melted butter
  • apply our seasoning mix of garlic powder
  • paprika
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Place some aluminum foil on a sheet pan or baking sheet and spray it with a little cooking spray
  2. Place our salmon filets on the sheet
  3. Brush with the melted butter
  4. season with our simple seasoning mix
