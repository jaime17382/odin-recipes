Lemon Pepper Skillet Chicken

Description

This is an easy to make Lemon Pepper Skillet Chicken recipe to follow that is quick and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Lemon Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Herbs De Provence
  • You'll also need a food thermometer (Make sure you are checking temperature constantly. Perfect temperature is 165 degrees Fahrenheit
  1. Heat pan on medium to high
  2. Season Chicken using the ingredients listed (wait to use Herbs de Provence until the end) and use a tiny bit of olive oil on each piece so that seasoning sticks better
  3. Once pan is hot enough and chicken is seasoned put a Tablespoon of butter in pan with a tablespoon of Olive Oil and let it melt
  4. Place chicken in skillet and let cook each side for 5 minutes
  5. Lower heat to medium-low and let sit for 3 minutes each side
  6. Lower heat all the way put Herbs De Provence on one side and let sit with top on for 3 minutes
  7. Flip chicken and do same thing for other side of chicken
  8. Make sure temperature is right and you're good to go!
